Biography

Muhammad Mobaidul Islam received his Bachelor of Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology in 2009. In 2018, he joined the North Carolina A&T State University to pursue his Ph.D. degree. His research interests include “Vision-based Control,” “Computer Vision,” “Pedestrian detection”, and “Self-driving cars”. He is a member of Autonomous Cooperative Control of Emergent Systems of Systems (ACCESS) Lab.
